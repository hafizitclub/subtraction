Learn: Two Methods to Subtract

Two Methods to Subtract 🔢
When we subtract teen numbers, we can use
one of two clever tricks!
Method 1

Subtract from Ones

14 − 3

The ones digit is big enough — just subtract from the ones!

Method 2

Subtract from Ten

15 − 8

The ones aren't enough — subtract from the 10 first!

🤔 How do I know which method?

Look at the ones digit of the teen number.
• If it is bigger than what you subtract → use Method 1
• If it is smaller → use Method 2

Method 1: Subtract from Ones 💜
Use this when the ones digit is big enough to subtract from.

Example: 14 − 3 = ?

Break 14 into 10 and 4.

Method 2: Subtract from Ten 💚
Use this when the ones digit is too small. Borrow from the 10!

Example: 15 − 8 = ?
